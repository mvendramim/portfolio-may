Ipad Magazine

This work was made in the Interaction Design class of the Graphic Design BFA at SUNY New Paltz. The goal was to create an interactive reading experience on the iPad for a non-fiction publication using the Adobe Publishing Suite. My choice was to do a travel guide to my hometown. It’s a work in progress.

Traveling Microsite

This work was made in the Web Design class of the Graphic Design BFA at SUNY New Paltz. The objective was to design and develop a microsite for a travel destination using CSS, HTML and Javascript. You can see the live version here.

Branding

This work was made in the Project class of the Graphic Design BFA at Federal Technological University of Paraná. The goal was to project the branding guidelines of a fictional restaurant. It is inspired by the movie Pulp Fiction.

Typographic Poster

This poster was made in the Typography class of the Graphic Design BFA at SUNY New Paltz. The goal was to design a poster based on a type designer. In my case, the chosen one was Jonathan Hoefler from Hoefler & Co. and I decided to use only typographic elements from his font families on it.

CD Package

This work was made in the Graphic Design class of the Graphic Design BFA at Federal Technological University of Paraná. The objective was to make a CD or DVD cover for a band, movie or game.

Printed Book Project

This book was made in the Graphic Design class of the Graphic Design BFA at Federal Technological University of Paraná. The main goal was to make its guideline, but also to understand mor about books’ layout and printing.
